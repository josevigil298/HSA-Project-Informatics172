Your Pathway

PHASE 1: TAX INDEPENDENCE TEST

Will you provide more than 50% of your own financial support (rent, food, insurance, etc.)? Identify your “Independent Tax Year”
Ensure your guardian does not claim you a dependent on their 1040 tax form

PHASE 2: INSURANCE COMPATIBILITY

Verify Plan Type: Is your current plan type a PPO, HMO, or HDHP? ⓘ
If you leave your current plan because you obtain health insurance through your employer, select an HDHP or HSA eligible plan

PHASE 3: THE PREP WORK

Deductible Buffer: Open a High-Yield Savings Account and save at least $1750 ⓘ
Keep a medical paper trail to track healthcare spending/needs

What is your plan type?

🩺
High-Deductible Health Plan (HDHP)

A health insurance plan with a higher deductible than traditional plans, you pay more out-of-pocket before insurance begins covering costs, but comes with lower monthly premiums.

📋
Other

Other health insurance plans such as HMOs (Health Maintenance Organizations) or PPOs (Preferred Provider Organizations).
